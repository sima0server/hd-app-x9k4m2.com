How Referral Profits Work

When you become an agent, every person who joins through your referral link becomes part of your team. You earn a commission every time any member of your team makes a deposit. The commission depends on their level in your referral tree.

Your Referral Tree

You (Agent) — the person who shares their referral link.

Level B: People who join directly through your link. You earn 5% of every deposit they make.

Level C: People who join through your Level B members. You earn 3% of every deposit they make.

Level D: People who join through your Level C members. You earn 2% of every deposit they make.

Commission Rates by Level

Level Relationship Commission Rate Example: TZS 100,000 deposit
Level B Direct referral 5% TZS 5,000
Level C Referral of Level B 3% TZS 3,000
Level D Referral of Level C 2% TZS 2,000

You earn commissions from all three levels at the same time. The more people in your tree who make deposits, the more you earn.

Real Examples with Team Levels

Example 1: Small Team

You have 3 Level B members, 2 Level C members, and 1 Level D member. Each deposits TZS 50,000.

Level B (3 × TZS 2,500): TZS 7,500

Level C (2 × TZS 1,500): TZS 3,000

Level D (1 × TZS 1,000): TZS 1,000

Your total: TZS 11,500

Example 2: Growing Team

You have 10 Level B, 8 Level C, and 5 Level D members. Each deposits TZS 100,000.

Level B (10 × TZS 5,000): TZS 50,000

Level C (8 × TZS 3,000): TZS 24,000

Level D (5 × TZS 2,000): TZS 10,000

Your total: TZS 84,000

Example 3: Large Team

You have 25 Level B, 20 Level C, and 15 Level D members. Average deposit TZS 200,000.

Level D (15 × TZS 4,000): TZS 60,000

Your total: TZS 430,000

Summary

Referral profits are earned on every deposit made by your team members. The larger your team and the more they deposit, the more you earn. You earn from all three levels at the same time — Level B (5%), Level C (3%), and Level D (2%).

  • Commissions are calculated automatically when deposits are approved.
  • You can track all your commissions in the Agent Bonus section.
  • Share your referral link to grow your team and increase your earnings.
