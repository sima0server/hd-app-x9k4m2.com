Your Three Levels

Registration Steps

  1. Agent shares referral link (e.g.,284756) via WhatsApp, SMS, or social media.
  2. Friend opens the link — agent info appears at the top. Referral code is locked.
  3. Friend completes form: Username, phone, password, country, region, birth year.
  4. CAPTCHA verification ensures a real person is registering.
  5. Profile picture — choose from preloaded avatars or upload a photo.
  6. Welcome bonus of TZS 1 – 50,000 is automatically added.
  7. Auto-login — redirected straight to the dashboard.

Summary

Referral profits are earned on every deposit made by your team members across all three levels — Level B (5%), Level C (3%), and Level D (2%). Commissions are processed automatically when deposits are approved by the admin.

  • Share your referral link to grow your team continuously.
  • Track all commissions in the Agent Bonus section of your Team page.
