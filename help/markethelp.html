What is HD Trading?

HD Trading lets you invest in trading packages. Each package contains multiple yield options. You pick a package, choose a yield, invest, and wait for the countdown to finish. Your profit is added automatically when the timer hits zero.

Key Terms

  • Package: A trading opportunity with yield options (e.g., Gold Rush Package).
  • Yield: The profit percentage you earn (e.g., 3.5% means TZS 3,500 profit on TZS 100,000).
  • Countdown Timer: Time left until the package settles and profits are paid.
  • Settlement: When your profit is automatically added to your balance.

How to Place a Trade (Step by Step)

  1. Tap the gold "Trade" button on any package card. A modal will slide up from the bottom.
  2. Select a yield percentage by tapping one of the options. The selected one highlights with a gold border.
  3. Enter your investment amount in TZS, or tap one of the quick buttons (10,000 / 50,000 / Max).
  4. Review the preview card — it shows exactly how much profit you'll make and your total return.
  5. Tap "Confirm Trade" — the amount is deducted from your balance and your trade is placed.

⚠️ Important

Trades are not instant. Your profit is paid when the countdown timer on the package card reaches 00:00:00. The timer shows exactly how long until settlement.

Understanding Settlement Timing

Every package runs on a 24-hour cycle. The countdown timer on each card tells you exactly when settlement happens:

  • Timer shows 12:00:00 → Your trade settles in approximately 12 hours.
  • Timer shows 00:30:00 → Your trade settles in just 30 minutes.
  • Timer shows 00:00:00 → Package expired. No new trades can be placed on it.

Settlement Example

You invest TZS 50,000 at 3.5% yield. The timer on the package card shows 4 hours remaining.

After 4 hours, when the timer reaches 00:00:00, your trade settles automatically:

TZS 50,000 (your money back) + TZS 1,750 (profit) = TZS 51,750

This amount is added directly to your balance. No action needed from you.

Beginner Tips

  • Start small: Try TZS 10,000 for your first trade to understand how everything works.
  • Watch the timer: Packages with less time remaining settle faster — you get your profit sooner.
  • Check the trend: Bullish (green) means prices are rising. Bearish (red) means prices are falling.
  • Use the quick buttons: Tap 10,000 or 50,000 for fast input instead of typing amounts manually.
  • Always review the preview: The preview card shows your exact profit before you confirm.
  • Check Market regularly: New packages are added throughout the day with fresh timers and yields.
  • Never invest more than you can afford: Trading carries risk — only use money you're comfortable with.
